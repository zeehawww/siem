Why This Is Novel

Commercial SIEMs like Splunk Enterprise Security, IBM QRadar, and Microsoft Sentinel surface raw alerts but require analysts to manually piece together the story of an attack. This engine automatically synthesizes a narrative per threat actor by correlating their alert sequence, detecting known MITRE ATT&CK chains, and writing a human-readable incident summary. This directly reduces Mean Time to Understand (MTTU) and analyst cognitive load.
