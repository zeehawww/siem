Pipeline Stages
  • Collect – read raw SSH auth logs from `logs/simulated_auth.log`.
  • Parse – normalize into JSON events with timestamp, user, IP & type.
  • Detect – apply rules such as brute‑force and sudo usage.
  • Alert – surface notable activity with severity labels.
