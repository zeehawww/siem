⚡ Markov Next-Step Prediction
Given an entity's most recent alert, a Markov transition matrix (derived from MITRE ATT&CK kill-chain co-occurrences) predicts their most likely next move with probability scores.
📊 Behavioral Entropy (Shannon H)
Computes H(X) = −Σ p·log₂(p) across each entity's event-type history. Low entropy = predictable (normal). A sudden spike means the entity is doing unusual things — indicator of compromise.
📈 Attack Velocity Profiling
Measures the rate-of-change and acceleration of attack events over 5 / 15 / 60 minute rolling windows. An ESCALATING profile means attacks are intensifying — requires immediate response.
⏱ Temporal Behavioral Fingerprinting
Quantizes inter-event time gaps (INSTANT <100ms → bot, FAST <1s → tool, HUMAN 1–30s → operator, SLOW >30s → stealthy probe). Same attack, different timing = different actor type.
